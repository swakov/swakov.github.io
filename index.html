Hotel Swakov

Located in the heart of Istria, Croatia's most wonderful region famous for its olive oils and windes, our hotel offers you and your family a perfect holiday opportunity.

Our guests spend their days swimming in the fine Adriatic Sea, sunbathing on our hotel's beautiful terrace, hiking through Istria's stunning terrain and dining in the finest Mediterranean restaurants.

Come and join us this July or August, and witness the beauty of Croatia.
