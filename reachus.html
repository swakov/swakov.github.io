How to reach us
Rent a car

Some of our guests choose to rent a car for the duration of their stay with us. If you so desire, you may rent small and large vehicles (for up to 8 persons) at Pula airport on the day of your arrival.

Public transport

Most guests do not rent a car. We recommend you take shuttle bus A22 from the airport into Pula city center. Let us know when you are coming and a friendly member of our hotel staff will greet you and walk with you to our location.
